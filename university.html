The university I attended in South Korea was called Hanyang University. Every summer since 1997, the school established the Hanyang International Summer School (HISS) as a way to encourage more international exchanges between Hanyang and its partner universities. Every summer, HISS offers more than fifty courses that are fully taught in English to internation students from all around the world.
